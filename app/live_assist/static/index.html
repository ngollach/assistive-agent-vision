Live camera demo for visually impaired users. The assistant periodically analyzes camera frames and speaks short safety-aware descriptions.

Safety notice: This is a prototype demo, not a certified navigation aid. Do not rely on it as your only source of guidance while walking. Use a cane, guide dog, trusted helper, or professional mobility support.
